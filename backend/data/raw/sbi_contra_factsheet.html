SBI Contra Fund

Source URL: https://www.sbimf.com/sbimf-scheme-details/sbi-contra-fund-12

As on: June 1, 2026

Investment Objective

To provide investors with opportunities for long-term growth in capital through a contrarian investment strategy. The fund invests in sectors and stocks that are currently out of favor or underperforming but have strong turnaround potential.

Scheme Highlights

  • Asset Class: Equity: Contra
  • Riskometer: Very High
  • Benchmark Index: S&P BSE 500 TRI
  • Lock-in Period: None (Open-ended scheme)

Investment Amounts

  • Minimum Initial Application (Lumpsum): Rs. 5,000 and in multiples of Re. 1 thereafter.
  • Minimum Additional Application: Rs. 1,000 and in multiples of Re. 1 thereafter.
  • Minimum SIP Amount: Rs. 500 (Monthly frequency) and in multiples of Re. 1 thereafter. Minimum SIP installments required is 6.

Expense Ratio (As of latest disclosure)

  • Regular Plan (TER): 1.90% per annum
  • Direct Plan (TER): 0.95% per annum

Exit Load Details

For exit load applicability, the holding period is calculated from the date of allotment of units. This fund offers a partial exemption for early redemptions.

  • For redemption/switch-out of units up to 10% of the initial investment value within 1 year (365 days) from allotment: Nil exit load.
  • For redemption/switch-out of units exceeding 10% of the initial investment value within 1 year (365 days) from allotment: 1.00% of the applicable NAV.
  • For redemption/switch-out of units after 1 year (365 days) from allotment: Nil exit load.

Fund Management
