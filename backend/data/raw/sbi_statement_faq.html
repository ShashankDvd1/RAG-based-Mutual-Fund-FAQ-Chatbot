SBI Mutual Fund Help Desk: Statements & Capital Gains Guide

As on: June 1, 2026

How to Download Account Statements

Investors can request and download their Statement of Account (SoA) through multiple official channels:

  • Online Portal (Website): Log into the SBI MF Investor Portal, navigate to the "Portfolio Summary" or "Reports" section, select the Folio Number and date range, and click "Download Statement of Account".
  • Missed Call Service: Give a missed call to +91-9121213344 from your registered mobile number. An SoA for all active folios will be sent to your registered email ID within 5 minutes.
  • SMS Service: SMS "SOA " to +91-9210122222 from your registered mobile number to receive the statement on your registered email.
  • WhatsApp Chatbot: Send "Hi" to +91-9240224444, select "Statement Request" from the menu, verify with OTP, and download the PDF.

How to Get Capital Gains Statements (Tax Documents)

Capital gains statements are essential for filing Income Tax Returns (ITR). To obtain them:

  1. Go to the official SBI Mutual Fund "Downloads & Reports" section at https://www.sbimf.com/smart-statement.
  2. Select "Capital Gains Statement".
  3. Enter your PAN or Folio Number.
  4. Select the Financial Year (e.g., FY 2025-26).
  5. Choose the format (PDF or Excel) and click "Email Me". The password-encrypted file will be sent to your registered email address within a few minutes.
  6. The default password to open the file is usually your PAN (in CAPITAL letters).

Updating Contact Details and PAN

To receive SMS/Email notifications and statements, ensure your contact details are updated. Updates can be done online via the portal after Aadhaar-OTP KYC verification or offline by submitting a physical KYC details modification form at any SBI MF Investor Service Center (ISC) or KFintech branch.
