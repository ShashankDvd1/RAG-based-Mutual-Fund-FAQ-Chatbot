SBI Liquid Fund

Source URL: https://www.sbimf.com/sbimf-scheme-details/sbi-liquid-fund-19

As on: June 1, 2026

Investment Objective

To provide reasonable returns combined with high levels of liquidity and safety of capital through investments in a portfolio of high-quality debt and money market instruments with maturity up to 91 days.

Scheme Highlights

  • Asset Class: Debt: Liquid
  • Riskometer: Moderate (Low to Moderate risk of principal)
  • Benchmark Index: NIFTY Liquid Index A-I
  • Lock-in Period: None (Open-ended scheme)

Investment Amounts

  • Minimum Initial Application (Lumpsum): Rs. 500 and in multiples of Re. 1 thereafter.
  • Minimum Additional Application: Rs. 500 and in multiples of Re. 1 thereafter.
  • Minimum SIP Amount: Rs. 500 (Monthly frequency) and in multiples of Re. 1 thereafter. Minimum SIP installments required is 6.

Expense Ratio (As of latest disclosure)

  • Regular Plan (TER): 0.30% per annum
  • Direct Plan (TER): 0.10% per annum

Exit Load Details

Exit load is applicable on redemption of units within 7 days from the date of allotment (including the day of allotment). No exit load is applicable from Day 7 onwards.

Fund Management
