SBI ELSS Tax Saver Fund (Formerly SBI Long Term Equity Fund)

Source URL: https://www.sbimf.com/sbimf-scheme-details/sbi-elss-tax-saver-fund-(formerly-known-as-sbi-long-term-equity-fund)-3

As on: June 1, 2026

Investment Objective

To provide investors with opportunities for long-term growth in capital under an Equity Linked Savings Scheme (ELSS) through active management of a diversified equity portfolio. The scheme offers tax deduction under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act.

Scheme Highlights

  • Asset Class: Equity: ELSS / Tax Saver
  • Riskometer: Very High
  • Benchmark Index: S&P BSE 500 TRI
  • Lock-in Period: 3 Years (Mandatory lock-in period from the date of allotment for each unit purchased).

Investment Amounts

  • Minimum Initial Application (Lumpsum): Rs. 500 and in multiples of Rs. 500 thereafter.
  • Minimum Additional Application: Rs. 500 and in multiples of Rs. 500 thereafter.
  • Minimum SIP Amount: Rs. 500 (Monthly frequency) and in multiples of Rs. 500 thereafter. Minimum SIP installments required is 6.

Expense Ratio (As of latest disclosure)

  • Regular Plan (TER): 1.70% per annum
  • Direct Plan (TER): 0.90% per annum

Exit Load Details

There is no exit load applicable for this scheme. Once the units complete the mandatory 3-year lock-in period, they can be redeemed or switched out with zero load charges.

Fund Management

Fund Manager: Mr. Dinesh Balachandran (Managing the fund since September 2016).
