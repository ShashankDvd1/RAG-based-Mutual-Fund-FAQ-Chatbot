SBI Small Cap Fund

Source URL: https://www.sbimf.com/sbimf-scheme-details/sbi-small-cap-fund-329

As on: June 1, 2026

Investment Objective

To provide investors with opportunities for long-term growth in capital along with the liquidity of an open-ended equity scheme investing predominantly in small cap stocks.

Scheme Highlights

  • Asset Class: Equity: Small Cap
  • Riskometer: Very High
  • Benchmark Index: NIFTY Smallcap 250 TRI
  • Lock-in Period: None (Open-ended scheme)

Investment Amounts & Subscription Constraints

Due to high inflows, SBI Small Cap Fund has restrictions on fresh subscriptions. Currently, lumpsum investments are suspended. Fresh investments can only be made through Systematic Investment Plans (SIPs) capped at Rs. 25,000 per PAN per month.

  • Minimum Initial Application (Lumpsum): Suspended (Lumpsum not allowed).
  • Minimum Additional Application: Suspended.
  • Minimum SIP Amount: Rs. 500 (Monthly frequency) and in multiples of Re. 1 thereafter, up to a maximum limit of Rs. 25,000 per month. Minimum SIP installments required is 6.

Expense Ratio (As of latest disclosure)

  • Regular Plan (TER): 1.80% per annum
  • Direct Plan (TER): 0.70% per annum

Exit Load Details

For exit load applicability, the holding period is calculated from the date of allotment of units.

  • For redemption/switch-out of units within 1 year (365 days) from the date of allotment: 1.00% of the applicable NAV.
  • For redemption/switch-out of units after 1 year (365 days) from the date of allotment: Nil exit load.
