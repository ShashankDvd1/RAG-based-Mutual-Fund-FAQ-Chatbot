SBI Large Cap Fund (Formerly SBI Bluechip Fund)

Source URL: https://www.sbimf.com/sbimf-scheme-details/sbi-large-cap-fund-(formerly-known-as-sbi-bluechip-fund)-43

As on: June 1, 2026

Investment Objective

To provide investors with opportunities for long-term growth in capital along with the liquidity of an open-ended scheme through an active management of a portfolio of large cap equity and equity-related securities.

Scheme Highlights

  • Asset Class: Equity: Large Cap
  • Riskometer: Very High
  • Benchmark Index: S&P BSE 100 TRI
  • Lock-in Period: None (Open-ended scheme)

Investment Amounts

  • Minimum Initial Application (Lumpsum): Rs. 5,000 and in multiples of Re. 1 thereafter.
  • Minimum Additional Application: Rs. 1,000 and in multiples of Re. 1 thereafter.
  • Minimum SIP Amount: Rs. 500 (Monthly frequency) and in multiples of Re. 1 thereafter. Minimum SIP installments required is 6.

Expense Ratio (As of latest disclosure)

  • Regular Plan (TER): 1.58% per annum
  • Direct Plan (TER): 0.82% per annum

Exit Load Details

For exit load applicability, the holding period is calculated from the date of allotment of units.

  • For redemption/switch-out of units within 1 year (365 days) from the date of allotment: 1.00% of the applicable NAV.
  • For redemption/switch-out of units after 1 year (365 days) from the date of allotment: Nil exit load.

Fund Management

Fund Manager: Mr. Milind Agrawal (Managing the fund since November 2023).
